easy jjajangmyeon!

Description

This recipe takes approximately 1 hour to prepare. It is a korean-chinese inspired dish and one of my favourites! You can use any vegetables or meat you want, depending on your dietary choices!

The ingredients you will need are:

  • jjajangmyeon noodles
  • meat of your choice (traditionally, pork belly)
  • 1 cup of korean radish
  • 1 and a half cups of onion
  • quarter cup of chunjang (Korean black bean paste)
  • 2 tablespoons of potato starch powder
  • water

Steps for cooking:

Tip: Whilst cooking your jjajangmyeon sauce, boil noodles at the same time to serve together!

  1. Stir fry meat in 1 tablespoon of oil for 4-5 minutes
  2. Add radish and stir fry for one minute
  3. Add onion and any other vegetables you want!
  4. Add chunjang and stir try for 1 minute to cook
  5. Add 2 cups of water and let simmer for 10 minutes with the lid closed
  6. Taste to check if vegetables are cooked. Also add in water and starch slurry until sauce thickens
  7. Add sesame oil and remove from heat
  8. Serve with jjaajangmyeon noodles and enjoy!
