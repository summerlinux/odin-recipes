insanely easy rice cooker meal!

Description:

There has never been an easier recipe than this. Literally just prepare your ingredients, put them in the rice cooker and wait!

The ingredients list will be things I frequently include in my own rice cooker meals!

Ingredients:

  • Frozen dumplings
  • Pak choi
  • Rice
  • Meat of any kind, normally beef
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oyster and soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

Steps to prepare:

Not manu steps, it's easy!

  1. Prepare vegetables and wash rice
  2. Add everything into the rice cooker, including seasoning
  3. Wait for it to cook
  4. Eat!
