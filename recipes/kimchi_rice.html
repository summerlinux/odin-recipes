kimchi fried rice!

This is a super easy dish to make and it uses up leftover rice! You can also use literally anything you have in your fridge in this dish and it will turn out delicious!

Ingredients you will need:

  • rice
  • kimchi
  • kimchi juice
  • water
  • sesame oil
  • any vegetable or meat of your choice!
  • sesame seeds (optional)
  • seaweed (optional)

Steps to prepare:

  1. Cook meat and vegetables in oil
  2. Add and stir fry your kimchi for 1 minute over medium heat
  3. Add rice, kimchi juice and water and cook for 7 minutes
  4. Add sesame oil and remove from heat
  5. Garnish with sesame seens and seaweed
  6. Optional step: I always have a fried egg with my kimchi rice, and it's amazing!
  7. Enjoy!
